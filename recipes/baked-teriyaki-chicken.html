Baked Teriyaki Chicken

Description

The Baked Teriyaki Chicken recipe from AllRecipes presents a delicious and easy way to enjoy a flavorful Asian-inspired dish at home. This recipe features chicken pieces marinated in a homemade teriyaki sauce made from soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, which infuses the meat with a sweet and savory profile. The marinade not only enhances the chicken's flavor but also helps to tenderize it, resulting in juicy and succulent pieces after baking.

Once marinated, the chicken is baked until perfectly cooked and caramelized, creating a delightful glaze that adds an appealing shine. The recipe includes simple instructions for preparing the teriyaki sauce and baking the chicken, making it accessible for cooks of all skill levels. Served with steamed rice and vegetables, this dish is a complete meal that brings a taste of the tropics to your dinner table. The Baked Teriyaki Chicken is not only quick to prepare but also a crowd-pleaser, perfect for family dinners or casual gatherings.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon cold water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Combine sugar, soy sauce, cider vinegar, cornstarch, cold water, garlic, ginger, and pepper in a small saucepan over low heat. Simmer, stirring frequently, until teriyaki sauce thickens and bubbles, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove from the heat.
  3. Place chicken thighs in the prepared baking dish. Brush both sides of each thigh with the sauce. Reserve any extra sauce for basting.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  5. Flip chicken and brush with sauce. Continue to bake, basting with remaining sauce every 10 minutes, until no longer pink and juices run clear, 20 to 30 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

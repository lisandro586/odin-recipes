Homemade Lasagna

Description

The Homemade Lasagna recipe from AllRecipes offers a classic Italian dish that is both hearty and satisfying. This recipe features layers of rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella, all nestled between sheets of tender lasagna noodles. The meat sauce is made with ground beef, onions, and garlic, simmered in a flavorful tomato sauce that brings depth and richness to the dish. The combination of herbs, including basil and oregano, adds an aromatic touch, making the sauce a perfect complement to the cheeses.

Assembling the lasagna is straightforward, allowing home cooks to create a comforting meal that can feed a crowd. The recipe provides clear instructions for layering the ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a delightful mix of flavors and textures. After baking, the lasagna is golden and bubbly, with a tantalizing aroma that fills the kitchen. This dish is ideal for family gatherings or special occasions, and it can be prepared in advance, making it a convenient option for busy weeknights. Enjoying a slice of this homemade lasagna is sure to bring warmth and satisfaction to any table.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
